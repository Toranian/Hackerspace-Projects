Tetris



Tetris is a tile-matching puzzle video game originally designed and programmed by Soviet Russian game designer Alexey Pajitnov.



The Tetris theme song (the default music in the GameBoy version) is an instrumental arrangement of a Russian folk tune called "Korobeiniki" (translation: The Peddlers), that tells the story of a meeting between a peddler and a girl, describing their haggling over goods in a metaphor for courtship. Here's are the first several lines of the song:


  • Oh, my crate is so full,
  • I've got calico and brocade.
  • Take pity, oh sweety,
  • Of this lad's shoulder
  • I will, I will go out into the tall rye,
  • I will wait there till the night comes,
  • Once I see the dark-eyed lass,
  • I will showcase all my goods.

The NES version of Tetris uses another Russian song for its default music: Dance of the Sugar Plum Fairy composed by Tchaikovsky.
